The Field Notes

One email a month. Cover reveals, release dates, the occasional process note. No spam, no churn.

If you read fiction the way I do — with one eye on the story and the other on how it was built — this newsletter is for you. Each issue takes one piece of the work in progress and opens it up: a research thread, a structural problem, a deleted scene, a cover study.

Subscribers see covers first, get release dates ahead of public announcement, and receive the occasional reader-only short story tied to a series.

By subscribing you agree to receive occasional emails from Collin Vaness. You can unsubscribe at any time.

Novelist writing thrillers and speculative fiction across military, science fiction, historical, and prehistoric epic. Based in Vancouver, BC.
