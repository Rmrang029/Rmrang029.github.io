Bios

Short · 25 words

Collin Vaness is a novelist writing thrillers and speculative fiction about civilizations under pressure and the people caught inside them.

Medium · 50 words

Collin Vaness writes thrillers and speculative fiction set at the edges of human endurance — from 12th-century Crusader Outremer to the deep prehistoric past to the wreckage of a near-future war. His novels span historical, military thriller, science fiction, and prehistoric epic, united by one question: what happens to people when the world they built breaks?

Long · 150 words

Collin Vaness is a novelist writing thrillers and speculative fiction across history, the near future, and the deep past. Blood and Faith: The Last Christian King of Jerusalem and The Gate of Bones (Book I of The Silent Threshold) are available now; five additional series are in active development across military thriller, science fiction, generation-ship saga, and 20-book space opera. A licensed professional engineer by training, he brings a working engineer’s discipline to research, operational detail, and the physics of his worlds.
